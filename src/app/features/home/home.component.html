Overview

The eUI Platform (eUI) is meant to ease development of Single Web Page Applications (SPAs) providing rich User Experience (UX) starting point to directly quick-start applications development.

It's also focusing on rationalisation and implementation of European Commission's User Experience (UX@EC) and the adoption of a common look and feel for your web applications.

Thereby, the eUI framework provides guidelines and recommendations on collaborative and reusable web components in order to help to build common and homogeneous graphical web interfaces that reflects the European Commission Style Guides towards a corporate look and feel. It also offers consistency from User Experience point of view through various participant projects such as Opsys, My Workplace, Compass Corporate, Assmal, eProcurement, Sygma, Sedia, etc.

The target audience goes from Developers who will find a lot of usefull technical information, but also Designers who want to reuse such materials to illustrate their work as well as Analysts and Managers to help them during conceptual phases.

Feel free to participate and help to improve the eUI platform.
